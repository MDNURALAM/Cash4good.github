FAQ

WHAT DO YOU DO

Sit back & relax we are the home & contents removal experts. Whether you’re having to deal with clearing a deceased estate, aged care facility or just looking to sell something we are here to help.

I feel overwhelmed & I don’t know where to start

Please don’t worry help is only a phone call away. We have the answers and can help you. Just call Darren and he’ll be happy to answer your questions.

What do you buy

We buy second hand furniture, white goods, Hi-Fi & record players, vintage & antique items, shed & garden items, collectables & memorabilia, rustic items, jewellery & old watches, 50’s 60’s & 70’s furniture, electrical & Bric-a-Brac. For obvious reason we cant buy all items but if your unsure we are only a call away or email cash4goodsperth@gmail.com

HOW DO YOU PAY FOR GOODS

We pay you cash in the hand for all your second hand furniture & household goods. However in some instances businesses and executors may require eft transfer with a receipt directly linked to a specified name.

DO YOU OFFER A FREE PICK UP SERVICE

Yes we travel to most Perth metropolitan suburbs at no additional cost to you.

WHERE ARE YOU LOCATED

We have a warehouse & located centrally in Carlisle so we can travel to most suburbs within the Perth metropolitan area.

DO YOU HAVE A SHOP

No we don’t have a shop, we are a warehouse operation. We on-sell most the items purchased in bulk. However we do have an Ebay store and sell items on our facebook page.

WHAT ARE YOUR TRADING HOURS

We pick up goods from around the Perth Metropolitan areas 6 days a week. Mon-Friday 8.30am to 5pm & Saturday 8.30am to 12pm

I LIVE OVERSEAS / LIVE TO FAR AWAY CAN YOU MANAGE MY JOB

Absolutely. A lot of the jobs we do is from special instructions. We have requests to remove items from deceased estates, rental properties or people who have moved interstate/overseas regularly. We even collect the keys from the agent and drop them back off.

DO YOU REMOVE GOODS FROM HIGH RISE APARTMENTS

Yes we do. However as you may understand these types of jobs are often difficult and time consuming & may also involve working in lifts. Working in lifts may often require following procedures such as prior bookings with the building manager to use the lift and specialised covers to wrap the lift walls to prevent damage. Costs may apply however these can be offset with the items we recover to cover our time and labour.
